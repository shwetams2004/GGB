About

 

We have all heard about the GGB which is known to be a symbol of the power and progress
of the United States. But it is also one of the most popular suicide destination in the world.
Every year around 30 people end their lives miserably in this bridge! In this article I will
discuss ways to avoid suicides by realising life and how to get going happily without
taking wrong decisions. I hope my article would help people who have such thoughts
and I really wish to draw them out of that devilish circle.

GGB Suicides

This bridge is known for it's history of deaths by suicides. Stories revolve about people who have
committed or attempted to commit or survived the fall! The first know case was of a World War-1
Veteran, Harold B. Wobber. It's pretty obvious why this man took this decision as he was a veteran!
But such excuses are for sure destructive. Several other people fell to their faitful death in the following
years. But there are some cases where survival was granted! Kevin Hines's story tells us what's in the
mind of a suicide survivor. "Jump now," said the voice in Kevin Hines's head. "And I did. I was
compelled to die." One feeling every survivor (and probably the victims too might) have felt when
falling through the sky is regret. The sense of regret ... regretting their life decisions, knowing what
they have done can not be take back was felt by them. Kevin Hines's story tells us how he felt from
the very second he plunged into air! It's aftermath and consequences are heartbreaking! Hines realized
that he made the greatest mistake of his life; so that you don't have to make the same!

Why People Consider Suicide As The Solution?

We all face difficult times in our lives. It's how we react to it that matters! Some of us overcome,
some ignore, some seek help, some forget, and some give up on life! If you go with the last option,
then this article is for you.When life hits you, hit it back with double impact! You are not in this
world to simply die and leave a void of your existence.When you feel hopeless, find hope in the
beautiful things that life provides. Look at the nature! It teaches us a great lesson how to overcome
difficulties and rise stronger that how you fell. Look at the life stories of people who are victims of
problems that they tried to escape, the only escape they found was not death! “The person who completes
suicide, dies once. Those left behind die a thousand deaths, trying to relive those terrible moments and
understand… why?”


Have you imagined how your loved ones would feel if you simply disappear one day?
Don't think about any of your negatives, toxic people, failure, bad vibes or anything that makes you gloom.
Instead think about the ones who pray for you everyday, your family, friends, your own great memories and
things that matter! You are here only once. So make it live longer; make it brighter! Like they say Place your hand
over your heart, can you feel it? That is called purpose. You’re alive for a reason so don’t ever give up..
You are here for a purpose, I'm here for a purpose. We are all here for a purpose! Don't ever lose faith over
problems that can be tackled in a different way; in the right way. If you ever think suicide is the solution, then
you are wrong. It's only destruction!

How To Help Yourself


If you understand the meaning of life and how many people wants you to be alive, you will never think
about killing yourself. You can help yourself by attending councelling if you are depressed or having any
thoughts of self-harm. Imagine what if tomorrow holds a different turn in your life and it all goes good
thereafter? Would you wait till tomrrow, or will you just leave without an end? Every unsolvable problem
in your life can be tackled in a matter of seconds! What you think would end your whole career could turn
out to be a blessing in disguise! If you are experiencing a toxic environment around some people, just remember
those people are none of your business! Just cut ties off the people who make you feel worthless. Ignore them
if you recieve hate; respect them if you get love. If you are worried about a particular incident, remember,
no one else is gonna keep it in their minds 'cause the world keeps going...and so do you. Anne Frank wrote:
“Think of all the beauty that's still left in and around you and be happy!”

Your Life is Precious!

We all have got just one life and that itself is short to an extend we fail to apprecite it.
Let's get with an example of King Solomon. King Solomon had a servant who admired him for
his wisdom. The servant noticed that when King Solomon gets angry or sad he looks at his
ring and the good mood returns. On the ring was written "this too shall pass" (in hebrew " Gam zeh ya'avor").
The ring reminded King Solomon that life is short and he needs to do his best to be in a good mood and help
his people. The realisation that anything in this world, be it good or bad, is all temprory. We need to keep moving.
Don't cling on to the past nor worry about the future. Everything happens for a reason! We need to appreciate life
in it's own way of happiness and suffering.
